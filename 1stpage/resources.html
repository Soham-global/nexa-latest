Resources for Families

Welcome to our resources page. Here you'll find a collection of helpful materials for parents and educators.

Helpful Links

  • Autism Speaks: A comprehensive resource for information and support for autism.
  • International Dyslexia Association: Offers resources and information about dyslexia.
  • NIH Learning Disabilities: Information about treatments and therapies for learning disabilities.
  • Understood: A platform providing support and resources for families of children with learning and attention issues.

Recommended Books

  • The Reason I Jump by Naoki Higashida: A memoir that offers insight into the mind of a young person with autism.
  • Overcoming Dyslexia by Sally Shaywitz: A comprehensive guide for understanding and addressing dyslexia.
  • Uniquely Human by Barry M. Prizant: A new understanding of autism from a personal perspective.

Videos and Webinars

  • Understanding Autism: A short video explaining autism and its characteristics.
  • Dyslexia Explained: An overview of dyslexia and effective teaching strategies.
  • Webinars for Parents: A series of informative webinars on supporting children with learning challenges.
